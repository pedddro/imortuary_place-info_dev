About Mount Sinai Hollywood Hills

Judaism has a long history of responding to the communal needs of its people and Mount Sinai Memorial Parks and Mortuaries continues to uphold this tradition by offering Jewish burial and funeral services to the community-at-large. The Jewish population of Los Angeles is indeed diverse; and although individual customs may be observed differently, all are treated with equal respect.

Mount Sinai Hollywood Hills truly is a park; a manicured urban setting that is both a cemetery and a sanctuary.

The landscape is quiet and unassuming, yet at the same time breathtaking. Driving through the gates, one passes sprawling hillsides, vibrant gardens, intricate mosaics depicting our Jewish heritage, and an array of trees specifically referenced in the Torah. There are places to sit, creating the opportunity for reflection, for taking in the beauty of the landscape and the depth of your feelings. The experience is seamless, always a place to park and an easy way of entering and leaving the property. The overwrought experience of visiting the departed is not marred by inconvenience.
